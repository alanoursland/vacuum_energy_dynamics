Case 4: Conformal-volume split check
========================================================================================================================
Given:

  gamma_ij = exp(2 zeta / 3) bar_gamma_ij
  det(bar_gamma) = 1

Then in 3 spatial dimensions:

  det(gamma_ij) = exp(2 zeta) det(bar_gamma)
                = exp(2 zeta)

Therefore:

  sqrt(gamma) = exp(zeta)
  zeta = ln sqrt(gamma)

This is a strong structural consistency check.

But it does not derive:

  source law for zeta
  B_s / F_zeta insertion dynamics
  boundary neutrality
  no-overlap
  residual-kill
  recovery coefficients
  parent equation
