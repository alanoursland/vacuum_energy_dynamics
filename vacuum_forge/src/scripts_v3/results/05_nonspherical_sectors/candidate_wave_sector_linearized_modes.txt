====================================================================================================================
Final interpretation
====================================================================================================================
The current reduced program does not yet contain gravitational waves.

Scalar A handles Newtonian/mass-flux physics.
Vector W_i is the natural home for frame dragging.
But radiative gravity needs a separate tensor TT sector h_ij^TT.

This is not a failure of the scalar branch; it is a degree inventory.
The next task is to decide whether the tensor sector is fundamental,
emergent from deeper vacuum variables, or absent and therefore fatal.
