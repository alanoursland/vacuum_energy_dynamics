============================================================================================
Final interpretation
============================================================================================
The v2 toy suite supports a sharper P7-style development target:

  The exterior functional should contain a kappa-suppression structure
  or exterior no-source condition such that kappa relaxes to zero.

  The source/interface should be allowed to seed the compensated/shear
  mode s, so the exterior can carry a gravitational distortion.

  Directly seeding exterior kappa is the failure mode: it produces
  AB = exp(2*kappa) != 1 and loses reciprocal scaling.

Continuum toy lesson:

  A bulk density like
      K_k (kappa')^2 + M_k^2 kappa^2
  with no kappa source has kappa=0 as a relaxed exterior solution.

  A bulk or boundary source for kappa moves the solution away from
  kappa=0 and therefore breaks reciprocal scaling unless another
  constraint removes it.

This remains a reduced-sector toy. The future theory still needs:

  - a covariant parent of kappa and s,
  - a source/interface rule,
  - a source law for the exterior profile,
  - and a full field equation or variational principle.
