Case 4: Symbolic idempotence check
========================================================================================================================
Formal projection law:

  P^2 = P

Applied twice:

  P(Pv) = P^2 v

If P^2 = P, then:

  P(Pv) = Pv

Formal before imposing idempotence: P*P*v
Formal target after idempotence:     P*v

Interpretation:
  Without a composition law such as P^2=P, a filter is not yet a projector.
