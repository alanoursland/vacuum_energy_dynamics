============================================================================================================
Candidate exact reduced source law:

  Delta_areal A = 8*pi*G*rho / c^2

where:

  Delta_areal A = (1/r^2)(r^2 A')'

Equivalently:

  d/dr(4*pi*r^2 A') = 4*pi*r^2 * 8*pi*G*rho/c^2

Define areal flux:

  F_A(r) = 4*pi*r^2 A'

Then:

  F_A'(r) = 32*pi^2*G*r^2*rho/c^2

[PASS] areal source law can be written as Gauss-flux law
