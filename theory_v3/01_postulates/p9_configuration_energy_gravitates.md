# P9: Configuration Energy Gravitates

## What This Postulate Says

The vacuum's configuration energy gravitates, with the same universal
per-unit-energy coupling as every other form of energy.

P4 commits the framework to configuration energy being real energy. P6
commits the framework to gravity acting uniformly per unit energy on every
form of energy. P9 closes the loop on the sourcing side: the configuration
energy of the vacuum is not exempt from the gravitational ledger it
defines. Curved configurations carry energy (P4), and that energy sources
further configuration, through exactly the coupling that any other energy
of equal magnitude would.

In compact form:

```text
Whatever gravitates is sourced by all energy, including the energy of
gravitation's own configuration, at the universal P6 rate.
```

This is the framework's self-coupling commitment, stated as ontology rather
than recovered as a coefficient.

## Epistemic Status

P9 is a structural postulate, adopted by theory-owner decision (2026-06-11)
following the Trial C2 bootstrap analysis.

It is strongly motivated but not strictly forced by P1, P4, and P6:

- P1 says vacuum is energy. P4 says configuration carries real energy.
  P6 says gravitational coupling is universal per unit energy -- but P6 as
  written governs how energy RESPONDS to gradients. P9 extends the same
  universality to how energy SOURCES configuration. The two directions are
  the two faces of the equivalence principle; committing to the second is
  nevertheless a new commitment.

External motivation: the Nordtvedt test. Lunar laser ranging shows the
Earth's gravitational self-energy (~4.6e-10 of its mass) both falls and
sources gravity like ordinary energy. A framework in which configuration
energy did not gravitate would fail this observation and would also break
its own T4/T9 chain (P8's self-coupling content is what produces beta = 1).

   -- the Newtonian field-energy convention, derived from inside the
   framework rather than imported.

P9 is the principle those results were conditional on.

## What P9 Fixes and What It Does Not

Precision matters here.

**P9 fixes:** that configuration energy sources gravity at all, and the
MAGNITUDE of its coupling (universal, |coupling| = 1 in the reduced
bootstrap normalization).

**P9 does not fix:** the SIGN of the configuration energy in any sector.
The temporal/static-sector sign (negative, lamda = -1) is selected by
exact exterior recovery, not by P9 alone. The radiative/shear-sector sign
remains a separate obligation (observed gravitational-wave energy is
positive; gate G03). The current working picture is therefore
sector-indefinite signature: temporal negative, radiative positive --
the same structure GR carries in its conformal-mode kinetic term.

temporal composition is the first-order shadow of the lamda = -1 bootstrap.
Under P9 plus exact recovery, the second-order temporal coefficient is
derived, not postulated. P8 is retained as a document describing the

selection. The previously unexplained linearity of the exact law in

**A K_GR selector route opens.** Taken to all orders covariantly,

framework-native seed of that argument, making "self-coupling consistency"
a candidate answer to the strain-functional question (K_strain frontier).

**A count-once rule is forced.** Configuration energy must be counted
exactly once -- inside the nonlinear geometry (GR-style) or as an explicit
divergence-safe source, never both. This is the cosmological-scale face of
the count-once discipline the field-equation search derived independently.

**Trial D's excess gravitates.** Any standing-frustration excess sources
gravity at the universal rate -- necessary for any dark-sector role, and
harmless to self-consistency at halo densities (self-coupling corrections
are O(Phi/c^2) ~ 1e-6 there).

## What P9 Does Not Do

It does not supply the covariant parent or the strain functional; it
constrains them.

It does not derive P7. Whether the same self-coupling principle forces the
spatial response (AB = 1) is the open spatial-sector bootstrap question
(Trial C3). If it does, both recovery postulates collapse into P9.

It does not fix sector signs (see above).

It does not resolve the interior. The bootstrap family's behavior at and
inside r = -lamda r_s is where the finite-admissibility principle (J_curv)
proposes to differ from GR; P9 is silent there.

It does not by itself decide the substance-vs-configuration bookkeeping of
P6 exchange during dynamics; it governs the static sourcing ledger.

## The Fence: What Counts as a Source Under P9

(Added at adoption, following multi-model review. This section is part of
the postulate's content, not commentary.)

P9 is a license with a strict admission rule, not a master key.

**The admission rule (the load-bearing clause):**

```text
A configuration-energy term is a gravitational source under P9 if and
only if it is part of the adopted dynamical law -- the vacuum action or
equilibrium equation -- at the current level of the theory. Its
stress/source contribution must then be counted, exactly once.

Source status is earned by presence in the dynamics, not by being
named an energy.
```

Operationally: a term gravitates because it survives variation of the
adopted action (or appears in the adopted equilibrium equation), never
because a document labels it "E_something."

**What P9 therefore does NOT mean:**

```text
It does not make diagnostic energies into sources. (e_curv,
  epsilon_vac_config, e_kappa, burden ledgers, and residual
  diagnostics remain accounting/diagnostic objects unless and until
  they earn a place in the dynamics.)

It does not license arbitrary curvature-energy reservoirs. (The FEC
  prohibitions on source reservoirs, repair currents, and
  relabeling-as-promotion stand unchanged; P9 is not an appeal route
  around them.)

It does not bypass the no-overlap/count-once, boundary-neutrality,
  mass-coin, or radiative-positivity gates. (A P9-counted source must
  still pass G02/G03, G09-G14; P9 changes what must be counted, not
  what must be proven.)
```

**The paradigm case (why C2 passes its own fence):** the (s')^2 term that
Trial C2 identified as self-coupling was not a named reservoir; it was
already present inside the framework's exact equilibrium equation
(Delta_areal A = 0). P9 required it to be READ as a counted source, with
the magnitude fixed by universality. That is the intended pattern: P9
forces the counting of energy the dynamics already contains; it never
invents energy for the dynamics to contain.

**Consequence for Trial D:** the standing-frustration excess gravitates
only once it holds a term in the actual vacuum dynamics. A name plus an
abundance estimate does not gravitate. This strengthens Trial D's entry
gate: before the equation-of-state question, the excess needs a seat in
the action.
