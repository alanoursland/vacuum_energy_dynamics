## What Was Clarified

### Equation elimination is allowed; equation selection is not

Group 42 opened the next phase of work as an exclusion map.

Candidate equation families may now be classified as:

```text
eliminated
rejected by guardrail
demoted
not well posed
conditional candidate
axiom required
future theorem target
```

This is progress because the field-equation system is underconstrained. The current strategy is to eliminate equations that violate known constraints before making any new axiom or branch choice.

But classification is not selection. A narrowed menu is not an adopted equation.

### Trace-normalization equation families were separated

Group 42 preserved the two branch-indexed trace-normalization candidate forms:

```text

  log(B_s_metric) = 2*zeta/d

scale branch candidate:
  log(b_s_scale) = zeta/d
```

These remain visible only as branch-indexed, non-active candidate forms.

The following families were eliminated or rejected:

```text

```

Exact trace-normalization declaration remains a later route requiring an explicit branch choice or explicit parallel declaration support.
