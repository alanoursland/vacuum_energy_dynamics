## What We Actually Learned

Group 98 clarifies that the determinant/Schur hierarchy currently has a safe but limited role.

It can support:

```text
finite hierarchy uniqueness;
nondegeneracy;
admissibility-chain stability;
exact numerator positivity as an algebraic theorem target.
```

It cannot yet support:

```text
a covariant J_curv definition;
a correction tensor H_curv;
an exchange tensor H_exch;
a total burden functional;
a source law;
a merger-energy prediction;
anti-singularity dynamics.
```

This is not a failure. It is a clean status correction.

## Strategic Interpretation

Group 98 is important because it protects the work from false confidence.

Groups 91–97 produced valuable mathematics. But Group 98 says exactly what that mathematics is allowed to mean right now:

```text
auxiliary admissibility support.
```

That is still valuable. A future source/functional derivation could upgrade the hierarchy into a physical theorem. But without that derivation, it stays a well-developed mathematical support branch.
