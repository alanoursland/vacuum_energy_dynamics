# candidate_insert_route_classifier — Result Note

## Result

The route classifier gives the main Group 55 classification.

Unsafe insertion families are rejected:

```text
direct trace/source/boundary/mass load routes;
double-entry and missing-entry trace routes;
source-carrying B_s/zeta/kappa routes;
scalar-tail, nonzero-flux, and shell routes;
mass-shifting trace charge routes.
```

Only a silent/inert route survives conditionally.

## Main Findings

The classifier successfully separates three statuses:

```text
rejected insertion families;
conditional silent/inert theorem target;
blocked physical use.
```

This is the key Group 55 result. The retained candidate is not dead, but all obvious physical insertion families are killed. The surviving route is extremely narrow and still requires:

```text
an insertion law;
residual/source/boundary/mass safety theorems.
```

The classifier also prevents premature active `O`: filtering does not show that the non-`O` route has failed.

## Boundary

No insertion route is opened. No field equation is constructed. No active `O`, recombination, or parent closure is licensed.

## Steering Consequence

Group 55 is close to summary. The summary should say: unsafe insertion families are excluded, only a silent/inert theorem-target route survives conditionally, and physical use remains blocked.
