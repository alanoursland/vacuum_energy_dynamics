# 50_symbolic_paired_trace_normalization_declaration_attempt_summary.md

## Result

Group 50 completed the symbolic paired trace-normalization declaration attempt authorized by Group 49. The result is not adoption and not field-equation use. It is a conditional, caveated, pre-adoption trace-normalization candidate.

## What Changed

Before Group 50, the paired trace-normalization route was attempt-ready with conditions. Group 50 took the next step and stated the actual symbolic paired declaration-attempt record.

The attempt carries both branch-indexed candidate expressions:

```text
metric-side candidate:
  log(B_s_metric)=2*zeta/d

scale-side candidate:
  log(b_s_scale)=zeta/d
```

These remain separate. The metric and scale sides are not collapsed into an unqualified `B_s`, a neutral `F_zeta` expression, or a compromise law. The factor-of-two burden remains visible.

The attempt also preserves symbolic mode. `zeta` remains a shared record-local trace-payload symbol. Symbolic `d` may be carried by the attempt. Numeric `d` remains conditioned and unfixed. The attempt does not choose `d=3` or any other numerical value.


These are not minor caveats. They define the narrow survival shape of the attempt.
