
## Main Findings

This script converts the Group 63 retention contract into concrete pass conditions.

The candidate cannot remain live just because the transition profile is elegant. A real origin has to explain why the profile or stress basis appears, why the energy-density relation is what it is, where the amplitude comes from, and why it does not hide source, trace, mass, or active-O load.

The rejected shortcuts are correct:

```text
profile by taste;
closure by taste;
amplitude by normalization.
```

Normalization can set a scale convention, but it cannot by itself derive physical amplitude.
