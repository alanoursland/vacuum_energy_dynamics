## Main Findings

The record must carry explicit no-insertion, no-active-`O`, no-residual/source-theorem, and no-parent-facing caveats. These are not merely general project guardrails; they are fields on the record surface.

## Boundary

Negative caveats are not positive theorems. Saying the record does not prove residual/source safety does not prove residual/source safety. Saying it is not parent-facing does not supply parent identity. The record remains pre-declaration infrastructure.

## Steering Conclusion

This result lets the paired record become useful without becoming dangerous. It is a better artifact precisely because it carries its own failure boundaries. Later scripts should preserve these caveats verbatim or treat any loss of them as an invalid upgrade.
