## Concrete Formula Route

Let:

```text
t = y^2
P_N(t) = 1 + sum_{j=1}^N a_j t^j
f = (1-y^2)^3
w = (1-y^2)^2
Xi = f P_N(y^2)
J = w dXi/dy
rho = dJ/dy
```

For moments:

```text
M_(2k) = ∫[-1,1] y^(2k) rho dy
```

integration by parts should give:

```text
M_(2k)
= 2k ∫[-1,1] y^(2k-2) (1-y^2)^4 [(2k-1)-(2k+3)y^2] P_N(y^2) dy
```

In `t=y^2` form define:

```text
I_k(P) = ∫[0,1] t^(k-1/2) (1-t)^4 P(t) dt
```

Then:

```text
M_(2k)=0
```

is equivalent to the moment-ratio constraint:

```text
I_k(P) = ((2k-1)/(2k+3)) I_(k-1)(P)
```

For:

```text
P_N(t)=1+sum_j a_j t^j
```

the `I_k` are Beta sums:

```text
I_k(P_N) = B(k+1/2,5) + sum_j a_j B(k+j+1/2,5)
```

Therefore the hierarchy coefficients are generated by the linear system:

```text
sum_j a_j [
  B(k+j+1/2,5)
  - r_k B(k+j-1/2,5)
]
=
r_k B(k-1/2,5) - B(k+1/2,5)

r_k = (2k-1)/(2k+3)

for k=1..N
```

This is the desired general formula.
