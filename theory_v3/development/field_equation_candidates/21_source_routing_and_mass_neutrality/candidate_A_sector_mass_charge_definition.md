## Exterior A-Sector Check

Use the reduced exterior A-sector solution:

\[
A(r)=1-\frac{2GM}{c^2r}.
\]

Then:

\[
A'(r)=\frac{2GM}{c^2r^2}.
\]

Therefore:

\[
F_A(r)=4\pi r^2A'(r)
=4\pi r^2\frac{2GM}{c^2r^2}
=\frac{8\pi GM}{c^2}.
\]

So:

\[
M_A(r)=\frac{c^2}{8\pi G}F_A(r)
=\frac{c^2}{8\pi G}\frac{8\pi GM}{c^2}
=M.
\]

The run recorded:

```text

```

This is the contentful reduced derivation of the A-sector reference mass charge.
