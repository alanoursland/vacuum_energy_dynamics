# 51_trace_normalization_adopt_defer_reject_decision_surface

## Purpose

Group 51 is the decision-surface group for the conditional symbolic paired trace-normalization attempt produced by Group 50.

Group 50 stated the paired attempt:

```text
log(B_s_metric)=2*zeta/d
log(b_s_scale)=zeta/d
```

and classified it as a conditional, caveated, pre-adoption trace-normalization candidate. It did not adopt Package B, choose a branch, make `B_s/F_zeta` insertable, construct active `O`, prove residual/source safety, open recombination, or open parent closure.

Group 51 should now ask the next honest question:

```text
Should the conditional symbolic paired trace-normalization attempt be adopted, deferred, or rejected?
```

This is not a physical insertion group and not a parent-equation group. It is a theory-decision audit. Its job is to make the adoption/defer/reject surface explicit and to prevent the conditional attempt from drifting into automatic adoption.

## Group Name

```text
51_trace_normalization_adopt_defer_reject_decision_surface
```

## Central Question

```text
Given the conditional symbolic paired trace-normalization declaration attempt from Group 50, what are the honest adopt/defer/reject options, and what evidence or obligations would each require?
```

## Desired Outcome

Group 51 should not merely say “not ready.” It should classify the decision surface sharply.

Allowed outcomes include:

```text
ADOPTABLE_ONLY_AS_CONDITIONAL_RECORD
DEFER_PENDING_SAFETY_THEOREMS
DEFER_PENDING_NUMERIC_D_SCOPE
DEFER_PENDING_BRANCH_CHOICE
REJECT_IF_ADOPTION_REQUIRES_INSERTION
REJECT_IF_NEUTRAL_COLLAPSE_REQUIRED
REJECT_IF_RECOVERY_SUPPORT_REQUIRED
ADOPTION_DECISION_REQUIRES_THEORY_OWNER
```

The best possible positive result is narrow:

```text
The conditional paired attempt may be adoptable only as a non-insertable,
pre-physical-use trace-normalization record, with caveats preserved.
```

The group may also decide that adoption should be deferred until residual/source safety theorems are attempted. That is allowed and may be the safest result.

## What This Group May Do

Group 51 may:

```text
inventory adopt/defer/reject routes,
define what adoption would mean at record level,
define what adoption must not mean,
classify adoption prerequisites,
test whether conditional adoption would force forbidden upgrades,

```

Group 51 may explicitly distinguish:

```text

parent-facing readiness,
```

```text

choose B_s_metric,
choose b_s_scale,

```
