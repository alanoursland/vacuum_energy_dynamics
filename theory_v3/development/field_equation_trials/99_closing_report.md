# 99 — Closing Report: The Field Equation Trials Are Over

**Closed:** 2026-06-12, by theory-owner decision, upon fulfillment of the
charter (`00_introduction.md`).

## Verdict

The charter asked for field equations. They were found:

$$G_{ab} + \Lambda g_{ab} = \frac{8\pi G}{c^4} T_{ab}$$

— Einstein's equations, derived from the adopted postulates (P1–P6, P7′,
P9) with zero matched coefficients, zero structural sectors, and every
candidate deviation killed at a named gate. The result is **admitted to
the theory** as `theory_v3/04_field_equations/` (statement of record,
derivation chain, sector architecture, divergences, obligations).

## One paragraph for the future reader

This program began with a stalled 103-group proof corpus, a solved-but-
unexplained projection hierarchy, and a theory owner who said "I don't
have field equations. I really don't know." It ended twelve days of
wall-clock time later (three sessions) with the equations derived and
the honest discovery that they were Einstein's all along — which the
owner had declared acceptable-but-not-the-goal at the outset. The goal
shifted accordingly: the vacuum, not gravity, is now the open question.
The method that got here — postulates with fences, gates with kill
conditions, machine-verified derivations, and the willingness to kill
six of one's own ideas — is the part most worth keeping.
