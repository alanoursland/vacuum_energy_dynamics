# Bondi-type mass-loss sign gate

A schematic Bondi-type loss law has the sign form

```text
dM/du = - k N^2.
```

The script verifies that positive news-squared flux lowers the Coulombic mass aspect in
this simple model. Radiative flux can change the charge over time, but the channel is
news-squared flux, not scalar moment-kernel charge insertion.
