# In D=4 EH is the unique dynamical Lovelock curvature term

in D=4, p=1 is dynamical, p=2 is topological, p>=3 vanishes.

In D=4:

```text
p=1 EH: dynamical
p=2 Gauss-Bonnet: topological
p>=3: vanishes
```

So EH is the unique dynamical Lovelock curvature branch in D=4, up to Lambda and boundary terms.
