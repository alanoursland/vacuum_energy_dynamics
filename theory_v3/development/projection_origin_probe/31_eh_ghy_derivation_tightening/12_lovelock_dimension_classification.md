# Lovelock order dimension classification

Lovelock terms vanish/topologize/become dynamical depending on D and p.

| D | p=1 | p=2 | p=3 |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 2 | topological | vanishes | vanishes |
| 3 | dynamical | vanishes | vanishes |
| 4 | dynamical | topological | vanishes |
| 5 | dynamical | dynamical | vanishes |
| 6 | dynamical | dynamical | topological |
| 7 | dynamical | dynamical | dynamical |

This is the Lovelock dimension/order gate.
