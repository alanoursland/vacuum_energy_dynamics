# y-Variable Beta Weight Origin

The factor `y^(-1/2)` is not a new physical field.  It is the Jacobian of the even-power projection variable `y=x^2`.

The base moment family has the form

```text
C_R[P] = ∫_0^1 P(y) (1-y)^(R+1) y^(-1/2) dy.
```

Thus the displayed weight already combines contact information and variable choice.
