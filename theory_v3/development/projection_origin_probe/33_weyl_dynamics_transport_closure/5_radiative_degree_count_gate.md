# 5. Radiative degree count gate

For a massless spin-2 field in `D` spacetime dimensions, the physical propagating count is

```text
D(D-3)/2.
```

Validated in `D=4`:

```text
4(4-3)/2 = 2.
```

Result: the radiative sector has two tensor polarizations in four dimensions. A scalar channel cannot supply the radiative degree count.
