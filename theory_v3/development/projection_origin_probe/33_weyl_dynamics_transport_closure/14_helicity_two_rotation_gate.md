# 14. Helicity-two rotation gate

The plus/cross polarization pair rotates by twice the physical transverse angle:

```text
[A_+', A_x']^T = R(2θ)[A_+, A_x]^T.
```

Validated checks:

```text
R(2θ)^T R(2θ) = I
R(2π) with θ=π gives identity in polarization space
```

Result: TT data transforms as spin-2/helicity-two tensor data, not as a scalar.
