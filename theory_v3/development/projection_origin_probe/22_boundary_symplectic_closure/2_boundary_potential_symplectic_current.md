# Boundary potential gives symplectic current

From `theta(delta)=p delta q`, the symplectic current is

```text
omega(delta_1, delta_2)=delta_1 p delta_2 q - delta_2 p delta_1 q.
```

The script checks antisymmetry under exchanging the variations.
