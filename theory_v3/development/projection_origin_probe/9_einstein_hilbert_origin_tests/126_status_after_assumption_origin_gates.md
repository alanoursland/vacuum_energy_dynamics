# Einstein-Hilbert Origin Tests: Status After Proofs 121-125

## What This Batch Adds

Proofs `121` through `125` test the assumption-origin gates that connect the
vacuum-energy ontology to the standard Einstein-Hilbert action.

Proof `121` validates:

```text

```

It still leaves open whether the vacuum ontology forces such an interval.

## Locality and Second-Order Gate

Proof `123` validates the variational derivative-order proxy:

```text

quadratic dependence on q'' -> fourth-order equation.
```

It also verifies that linear second-derivative terms can be boundary-shifted
into first-derivative strain.

```text
-q q'' + d(q q')/dx = (q')^2.
```

The boundary term removes derivative-of-variation boundary data, matching the
role of EH/GHY boundary bookkeeping.
