# Matter Ontology Gap Audit — Proof Chain Plan

## Purpose

The previous `matter_action_origin_gate` closed a conditional route:

```text
if matter actions use the shared metric interval and volume structure,
then metric variation produces stress-tensor source coupling.
```

This folder audits what that result does **not** derive.

The target is not to weaken the stress-coupling result. The target is to keep
its scope exact:

```text
metric interval dependence -> source route;
not -> existence, species, charges, masses, spin, gauge sectors, discreteness,
or quantum matter ontology.
```

## Strategy

Each proof script gives a small algebraic witness showing that some matter-side
information is either imported, degenerate under stress projection, or requires
an additional field/channel.

The group should close the following claim:

```text
The metric stress tensor is a universal source ledger once matter action exists,
but it is not an ontology generator for matter itself.
```

## Batches

### Batch A — Conditional stress route

1. Shared metric variation conditional gate.
2. Stress variation is many-to-one.
3. Mass parameter is imported.
4. Species count is imported.
5. Additivity does not determine decomposition.

### Batch B — Missing internal structure

6. Charge/gauge coupling is independent of metric stress route.
7. Spin current is not determined by symmetric stress.
8. Internal phase/gauge labels are invisible to stress.
9. Trace and energy density do not recover equation of state uniquely.
10. Null/traceless matter still gravitates through full stress.

### Batch C — Source conservation and positivity limits

11. Diffeomorphism conservation is conditional on equations of motion.
12. Conservation does not imply matter existence.
13. Positivity is an extra energy condition, not a metric-variation theorem.
14. Compact support/localization parameters are imported.
15. Discreteness/particle number is not derived by stress coupling.

### Batch D — Quantum and ontology frontier

16. Quantization is not produced by classical stress variation.
17. Gauge charge conservation needs internal symmetry.
18. Matter action reconstruction is underdetermined by stress.
19. Dependency table.
20. Conclusion.

## Expected conclusion

This folder should conclude:

```text
Shared interval dependence explains the universal source route once matter is
present. It does not derive matter's microscopic ontology.
```

The next real frontiers remain:

```text
matter existence,
species/internal symmetry,
mass and charge spectra,
spin/statistics,
quantization,
and possible vacuum-configuration origin of matter actions.
```
