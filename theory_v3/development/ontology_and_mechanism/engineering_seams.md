energy at the universal coupling; expect c^4/G-class taxes typical of all
metric engineering. Computing the leak-per-watt figure of merit is a
well-posed future calculation.

**Gates that bound it:** G21 (Cassini) bounds ambient leaks, not local
engineered ones; detection of an engineered leak needs only local
metrology (clock/ruler anisotropy).
