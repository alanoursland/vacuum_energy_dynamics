## Central Claim

If space is a discrete packing, cosmic expansion admits exactly two
readings, and existing data decides between them:

```text
STRETCH   fixed graph; cells dilate with a(t)      -- EXCLUDED, twice
GROWTH    fixed cell scale; new cells, N ~ a^3     -- survives, and
                                                       is self-funding
```

The two readings are permanently indistinguishable to the metric
sector (same volume history, same dust stress, same Einstein
equations). They are nonetheless decided by side effects on locked
quantities:

```text
KILL 1  the microphysical length calibrating Newton's constant grows
        with a(t) under stretch: Gdot/G = -H ~ -7e-11/yr, vs the
        lunar-laser-ranging bound ~1.5e-13/yr. Excluded ~480x.
KILL 2  dilation-invariant cell energies make the stretched vacuum
        floor pressureless (w = 0) at ~Planck density, and a diluting
        density is not sequestered: overclosure by ~1e122.
SELF-FUNDING  under growth, the intensive floor has p = -u (w = -1
        exactly), and the creation cost u dV equals the floor's
        negative-pressure work term by term: the first law funds
        expansion.
FALSIFIER  the surviving reading has NO G-drift mechanism at all:
        Gdot = 0 exactly, pre-registered, and under genuine pressure
        (H0 sits ~480x above current bounds, which tighten).
```

## Abstract Skeleton

If the gravitational vacuum is granular, cosmic expansion must be one
of two things: the stretching of a fixed microstructure with the scale
factor, or the creation of new microstructure at fixed scale. The two
readings produce identical metric physics and identical cosmological
histories, and so appear observationally indistinguishable. We show
they are not. In a packing model of the vacuum in which Newton's
constant is calibrated by the microstructural length scale, stretching
forces G to drift at the Hubble rate, excluded by roughly two orders
of magnitude by lunar laser ranging; independently, stretching turns
the vacuum's ground-state energy from a constant (sequestered) density
into a pressureless component at near-Planck density, overclosing the
universe by ~1e122. The surviving reading -- space grows -- is exactly
self-funding: the ground-state energy's w = -1 equation of state pays
the creation cost term by term through the first law. The reading
commits the model to Gdot = 0 exactly, a pre-registered falsifier
sitting roughly a factor 500 beyond current bounds and approachable by
foreseeable improvements in ranging and timing data.

## Section Plan (letter format -- ~4-6 pages)

### 1. The Dichotomy

- One-paragraph model import (cite paper 8): edge-length graph, flat
  cells, hinge curvature, frustrated floor, floor-Newton lock.
- Stretch vs growth defined; the equivalence of their metric faces

  looks metaphysical but is not.

### 2. Kill 1: Stretch Drags Newton's Constant

- The lock: G ~ 1/(f'(Delta_0) l). What is held fixed and why the
  calibration length is the comoving cell edge under stretch (the
  step a referee will push on -- write it carefully).
- Gdot/G = -H; numbers; the strongest published Gdot bound with
  proper citation (survey LLR, planetary ephemerides, pulsar timing,
  BBN during drafting; quote the best, not the roundest).

### 3. Kill 2: Stretch Un-Sequesters the Floor

- Dilation invariance of cell energies (cite the measured result);
  E const + V growing => p = 0, w = 0.
- Why sequestering protects constant DENSITY only; the diluting floor
  gravitates; the 1e122 arithmetic.
- Note the architectural stakes: this kill protects the w = -1
  prediction of the companion Lambda paper.

### 4. The Survivor: Creation, Self-Funded

- l const => G const exactly.
- Intensivity (measured) => E = uV => p = -u: w = -1 at the substance
  level.
- The identity: creation cost u dV = negative-pressure work -p dV.
  The first law funds expansion; no reservoir, no debt.

### 5. The Falsifier and the Outlook

- Gdot = 0 exactly, forever: no drift mechanism remains. Distinctive
  against scalar-tensor/varying-G rivals (they generically need
  drift).
- The pressure statement: H0 / (current bound) ~ 500 and closing.
- Outlook paragraph (one only): the suppression clause
  C = 3H(1 - alpha delta) -- exactly background-invisible for any

## Writing Notes

- The letter's charm is the shape: an apparently metaphysical
  question decided by benchtop-grade data. Keep the prose at that
  register -- no ontology defense, no postulate stack.
- State generality honestly: any granular-vacuum model whose G is
  calibrated by the grain scale faces this dichotomy; ours is the
  worked example.
- The self-funding identity is the emotional payoff -- give it its
  own displayed equation and one sentence of plain speech ("the
  first law funds expansion").
- Length discipline: if it exceeds 6 pages, cut the outlook, never
  the kills.
