# Appendix B. Sample Adoption Record Template

```text
Adoption ID:

Statement adopted:

Human owner:

Timestamp:

Reason for adoption:

Scope:

Fences:

What this does not license:

Expected downstream uses:

Possible appeal conditions:

Possible empirical or internal kill conditions:

Related probes not adopted:

Supersession policy:
```

The record should make it impossible to confuse the adoption with a derived
theorem. If later work derives the adopted content, the original record should
be superseded rather than erased.
