# Appendix A. Sample Charter Template

```text
Title:

Question:

Motivation:

Allowed assumptions:

Forbidden assumptions:

Candidate object:

Required derivation artifacts:

Required data artifacts:

Verdict states:
  PASS:
  KILLED:
  UNDERDETERMINED:
  NOT_READY:
  HANDOFF_READY:

Kill conditions:

Dependencies:

Owner confirmation required before gate construction:

Handoff conditions:
```

The charter should be written before the derivation begins. If the question or
candidate changes, write a new charter or explicitly amend the old one.
