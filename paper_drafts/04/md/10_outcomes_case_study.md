# 10. Outcomes of the Case Study

The workflow produced three classes of outcome.

First, it killed mechanisms. Candidate routes including tidal vacuum
nucleation, depletion-history dark matter, burden-reduction forces, explicit
source placement of configuration energy, boundary smoothing, Weyl-class
quadratic curvature, and scalaron-mediated gravitational Yukawa corrections
failed their gates or were fenced out.

Second, it made adoptions visible. P9 and P7-prime became explicit
human-owned inputs rather than hidden assumptions. Their downstream
consequences were then auditable.

Third, it closed the gravitational sector on GR:

```text
G_ab + Lambda g_ab = (8 pi G / c^4) T_ab .
```

This is the strongest process-quality result. A sycophantic workflow would
likely have preserved exciting deviations. This workflow killed them. The
surviving theory currently predicts no accessible gravitational deviation from
GR in the closed sector. Its remaining open work lies in rigor closure,
vacuum-sector physics, the origin of `Lambda`, dark-sector mechanisms, and
non-gravitational tests.

The methodology lesson is that killed ideas are not embarrassment artifacts.
They are evidence that the workflow had teeth.
