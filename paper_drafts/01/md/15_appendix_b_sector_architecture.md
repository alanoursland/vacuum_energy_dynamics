# Appendix B. Sector Architecture

The sign structure is:

```text
static scalar/shear:  negative, constraint
TT radiation:         positive, propagating
vector:               constraint-sourced by momentum
trace/kappa:          suppressed in static vacuum
```

The crucial point is that negative energy is acceptable only because it is not
a propagating reservoir. Promoting the static scalar to a wave gives an
unbounded Hamiltonian. P5 therefore forces the static sector to remain
elliptic.

Source-free uniqueness prevents extraction of negative energy from empty
space:

```text
(r^2 A')' = 0,
A -> 1 at infinity,
regularity
=> A = 1 .
```

The detailed proof belongs primarily to Paper 2, but this appendix should
retain the compact theorem statements for readers of the flagship paper.
