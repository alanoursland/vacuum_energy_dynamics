# 12. Conclusion

The derivation fixes GR rather than a deviation from GR. Starting from a
vacuum-substance ontology, static energy bookkeeping gives the Schwarzschild
exterior and a negative local static field energy. Stability cages that sector
as a constraint. Spin-2 closure and P9 self-coupling fix the dynamical
normalization. P7-prime eliminates the only healthy local four-derivative
scalar. The remaining local gravitational response is the Einstein equation
with a cosmological term.

The result is not the end of the theory. It is the closure of its gravitational
sector. The remaining work is sharper because the gravitational freedom has
collapsed: derive `Lambda`, develop the vacuum sector, complete covariant rigor
debts, and keep the null-test ledger honest.

The strongest lesson is negative as much as positive. Candidate deviations
died: propagating negative-energy scalar modes, boundary smoothing, scalaron
hair, and gravitational-strength Yukawa corrections. The theory survived by
becoming more like GR in its tested gravitational sector, while retaining a
different ontology and a narrower set of future targets.
