## Abstract Skeleton

We show that a vacuum ontology in which spacetime is identified with a
substance of constant local density forces the unimodular constraint
on the gravitational measure, and we develop the consequences. The
cosmological constant enters only as an integration constant of the
trace-free field equations; constant shifts in vacuum energy density
are exactly sequestered from gravity, dissolving the
radiative-stability face of the cosmological constant problem; and the
tempting intermediate route -- deriving Lambda's value as a partial
relaxation of the vacuum's ground strain -- is closed by an exact
relief function that is quadratically flat about the ground state. The
surviving structure makes one sharp, currently-tested prediction: the
dark-energy equation of state is w = -1 exactly, at every redshift,
with no drift. We state the kill condition against evolving dark
energy and the discipline rule that forbids absorbing an adverse
result into auxiliary components. All derivations are machine-verified;
the verification archive is cited per theorem.
