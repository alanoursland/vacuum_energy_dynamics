# Appendix E. Provenance Note

This paper was extracted from a larger theory-development program.  The
original coefficient appeared inside a projection hierarchy motivated by
boundary reduction.  Subsequent symbolic probes showed that the coefficient is
not specific to that physical setting: it is the base element of a weighted
moment/contact ladder.

This manuscript deliberately removes the physical interpretation.  Its claims
are mathematical:

- the survivor ratios form a beta-moment ladder;
- the index `R` records endpoint contact order;
- the finite projection systems are cross-Gram systems;
- invertibility follows from nondegenerate pairing, not determinant
  positivity.

Any later use of this ladder in a physical theory must supply an independent
selection argument for the relevant contact level, domain, and weight.
