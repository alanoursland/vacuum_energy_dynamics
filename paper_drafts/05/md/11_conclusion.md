# 11. Conclusion

We have isolated the mathematical structure behind the survivor-ratio ladder

```text
r_{R,k} = (2k - 1)/(2k + 2R + 3).
```

The coefficient is the adjacent moment ratio of a beta-weighted endpoint
functional.  The index `R` records endpoint contact order, equivalently the
primitive power `m = R + 2` in the `x` variable.

The associated finite projection matrices are cross-Gram matrices between two
admissible bases.  Their invertibility follows from nondegeneracy of a
positive weighted pairing and invertibility of the basis change.  Determinant
positivity is not required.

The paper leaves three tasks before release: a literature search in weighted
moment and Gram determinant theory, a notation-clean all-orders basis lemma,
and a final verification appendix that points directly to reproducible
scripts.
