# 9 · The Graveyard: Ideas We Killed Ourselves

[← TOC](README.md) · [prev: Predictions](08_predictions.md) · [next: Open Problems →](10_open_problems.md)

If you suspect motivated reasoning, start here. A theory's credibility
lives less in what it claims than in what it was willing to kill. This
program's house rule: every mechanism gets a kill condition *before* it
gets a claim, and an executed kill is recorded with the same care as a
success. These are the major graves, each with its verified death
certificate.

## The partial-relief route to Λ (killed 2026-06, derivation 037)

**The hope:** the vacuum's frustrated ground state relieves a tiny
fraction of its strain as residual curvature, and *that* is the
observed cosmological constant — Λ's value derived from frustration.

**The kill:** the relief function is exact and computable:
cos δ(s) = cos s/(1 + 2cos s), giving Δ(s) = Δ₀ − (5√2/24)s². It is
*quadratically flat* about the ground state. Producing the observed Λ
would relieve ~10⁻¹²² of the frustration, and no mechanism selects
such a relief — there is no intermediate regime. The value route from
floor to Λ is dead, permanently.
[Proof →](11_proof_index.md#lambda)

## Our own scalaron-cancellation mechanism (killed 2026-07-02, derivation 047)

**The hope (ours, proposed in-house):** the packing's R²-class
correction seems to violate P7′'s "four-derivative sector exactly
empty" — but perhaps the unimodular constraint kills the scalaron,
dissolving the tension for free.

**The kill (also ours, same week):** the f(R) field-equation tensor is
identically conserved, so the Bianchi identity *reconstructs* the
trace equation from the unimodular system — the very mechanism that
makes Λ an integration constant resurrects the scalaron, mass
m² = 1/(6α), intact. We refuted our own proposal and recorded it as a
refutation. The tension was then resolved honestly by a scoping ruling
(P7′ exact in the continuum idealization; the Planck-range scalaron a
controlled correction ~10³⁰ below laboratory reach), not by the
cancellation we had hoped for.
[Proof →](11_proof_index.md#packing)

## The engineered-boundary energy release (killed 2026-07-02, derivation 053)

**The hope — the founding dream of this entire research program:**
arrange matter into boundaries that confine the vacuum into quasi-2D
packing (2D triangles tile perfectly; no frustration), letting the
confined region relax below the 3D floor and release the difference —
a Casimir-like tap into the vacuum's stored energy.

**The kill:** under the adopted microphysics the operator became
derivable, with *no free coefficient* — the same lock that calibrates
Newton's G fixes the released pressure at ~10¹¹² Pa (Planck packing).
Casimir-class experiments bound anomalous plate pressures at ~10⁻² Pa.
Any coupling that would let matter boundaries confine the packing is
excluded by **54–114 orders of magnitude**. Matter cannot act as walls
on the vacuum — consistent with matter being strain *in* the packing.
The founding energy-extraction hope is closed at leading order, killed
by its own long-awaited formula. (The thermodynamics was never the
problem — a closed cycle nets exactly zero; it died of magnitude.)
[Proof →](11_proof_index.md#packing)

## The stretch reading of expansion (killed 2026-07-02, derivation 054)

**The candidate:** cosmic expansion = a fixed vacuum graph whose cells
dilate with the scale factor.

**The kill, twice over:** (i) the microstructural length calibrates G,
so stretching forces Ġ/G = −H — excluded ~480× by lunar laser ranging;
(ii) dilation-invariant cell energies make the stretched floor
pressureless (w = 0) at Planck density, unprotected by sequestering —
overclosing the universe by ~10¹²². Space grows; it does not stretch.

## Also buried, more briefly

- **Finsler-inside-the-metric:** the edge-length state space stores
  *exactly* a metric per cell (10 edges = 10 components in 4D; a
  quartic norm needs 35). Not suppressed — unstorable. (049)
- **Every pre-packing route to nonzero Λ:** variational, boundary,
  topological, measure-identity, relaxation — a nine-derivation sweep
  (008–016), all negative, later understood as *theorems* of the
  unimodular reading.
- **Scalar and vector gravitational radiation:** killed in the
  field-equation closure; only the two tensor polarizations survive.
- **"The vacuum's floor gravitates as dark energy":** sequestering
  forbids it — the floor contributes exactly zero. ([Λ →](06_lambda.md))

## Why this chapter exists

Every one of these was *our* idea, and several were beloved — the
boundary-release mechanism was the reason the theory owner started
this work. They died because the rules were set before the answers
came in. The same rules currently expose the theory's surviving
claims to live experiments ([Predictions →](08_predictions.md)); when
one of those dies, it will be recorded here with the same plainness.
